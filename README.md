# CRM Sales & Lead Analysis Dashboard

## Project Description
This project analyzes CRM sales and lead data to evaluate business performance, track sales opportunities, and identify factors influencing lead conversion and revenue growth. The dashboard provides insights into lead sources, industry performance, opportunity conversion rates, and expected revenue trends to support data-driven decision making.

---

## Key Metrics
- Total Leads: 10,000
- Total Lead Amount: ₹57.11M
- Expected Opportunity Revenue: ₹184.14M
- Active Opportunities: 78
- Opportunity Conversion Rate: 31.06%
- Lead Conversion Rate: 10.16%
- Win Rate: 42.77%
- Loss Rate: 41.56%

---

## Key Insights
- The Safety & Security industry generates the highest number of opportunities and expected revenue.
- Closed lost deals are higher than closed won deals, indicating potential inefficiencies in the sales process.
- Inside Sales and Website channels generate the most leads.
- Lead generation increased significantly after 2019, indicating growth in marketing activities.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Improve lead qualification and follow-up strategies to increase opportunity conversion.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing industries such as Safety & Security and Life Sciences.
- Increase investment in digital marketing and inside sales channels.
- Analyze lost deals to understand customer objections and improve sales strategy.
